Dikir Barat

  • Traditional musical performance originating from Kelantan.
  • Features a group of singers and musicians performing in a call-and-response style.
  • Incorporates energetic hand movements and synchronized clapping.
  • Often includes humorous and satirical elements in the lyrics.

Mak Yong

  • Traditional dance-drama from northern Malaysia, particularly Kelantan.
  • Combines acting, singing, and dancing to tell stories from local mythology.
  • Recognized by UNESCO as a Masterpiece of the Oral and Intangible Heritage of Humanity.
  • Performances often involve elaborate costumes and live music.

Wayang Kulit

  • Traditional shadow puppet theatre from Kelantan.
  • Uses intricately carved leather puppets to depict stories from folklore and mythology.
  • Accompanied by traditional music and narration by a puppet master (dalang).
  • Performances are usually held at night and create a magical, immersive experience.
